How It Works

Plug In Your Device

Connect the Offline device to any computer or mobile device. It's a self-contained system that requires no installation or internet connection to get started.

02

Ask Anything

Open the secure, local interface and start your query. You can ask complex questions, request summaries, analyze data, or even ask it to write code for you.

03

Get Instant, Private Answers

Receive answers and insights in seconds. Since everything runs locally, your queries are processed instantly and with complete privacy. Your data never leaves the device.
